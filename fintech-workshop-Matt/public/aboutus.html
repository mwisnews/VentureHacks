About CreDucks

CreDucks is a financial consulting tool created by Gil Austria, Nicole Crockett, Matthew Wisnewski, and Sara Zakrinski, four students from Stevens Institute of Technology.

This tool will be used by banks who are looking to help small businesses build their credit.

This web app takes in financial information from the business owner and returns information regarding where their money should be going and how much in each section.
